About Us

“Established in 2007, “Chicago Onyx Dance Alliance,” otherwise known as CODA, was formed as a merger of two companies that shared the same passion for dance and decided pursue their artistic capabilities under one roof. With their main focus aimed at developing dancers of both trained and untrained backgrounds to the fundamentals of hip hop, they quickly became one of the most prominent and entertaining dance companies in the Chicagoland area. Since then, CODA has continued to share their love of dance to their surrounding communities, as well as participating in some of the industry’s most well-known competitions, including World of Dance Chicago, Prelude Midwest, Hip Hop International, and World of Dance Finals. As a multicultural dance company, CODA prides itself on not only representing a versatility of dance styles as a team, but they also demonstrate the importance of growing in their craft as a family. By having a deep appreciation for the art of dance, and demonstrating a strong work ethic from our members, CODA hopes to keep inspiring others of the potential that dance has to make a positive impact in our communities. ”
